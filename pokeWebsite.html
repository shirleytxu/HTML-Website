✧ Pokemon #1: Eevee ✧

Dex Entry #133: The Evolution Pokemon

Eevee is so cute and fluffy, as adorably depicted in the GIF above- what more could you want? Try to decode this for a fun fact about Eevee! ↓

Eevee can evolve into 8 Pokemon!

This is what the riddle said: "Eevee can evolve into 8 Pokemon!" As you may or may not know, Pokemon can only evolve into one other Pokemon. Eevee's not the Evolution Pokemon for no reason! Eevee's evolutions (or eeveelutions) are all equally cute-- read more about them here!

Mouse over me for a list of Eeveelutions!

Eeveelutions: Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Leafeon, Glaceon, Espeon, Umbreon, Sylveon

✧ Pokemon #2: Zoroark ✧

Dex Entry #571: The Illusion Fox Pokemon

Click the Zoroark below to go to it's Bulbapedia Page!

While I mainly like Zoroark for its sick looking black-red color scheme and overall design, Zoroark also earns some pretty neat kudos points for the following:

  1. Taking great care of its (adorable) pre-evolution, Zoroa
  2. Being extremely powerful!
  3. Its ability to transform into ANY Pokemon it wants- isn't that sooooo cool?!

I'd say those are a couple solid reasons to like Zoroark! Onto the next Pokemon!

✧ Pokemon #3: Lucario ✧

Dex Entry #448: The Aura Pokemon

Lucario also has a Mega-Evolution (essentially a stage where the Lucario can evolve another more powerful stage if the bond it shares with its trainer is strong enough). Lucario is depicted to the right, while Mega-Lucario is depicted to the left.

As you can see from the above image...

[Lucario] has developed four black appendages that hang down from its head, which rise when Lucario reads or manipulates aura, a special energy that it senses. Lucario can study this energy to predict the movements of their opponents and track their quarry. They also have the power to manipulate this energy in an offensive manner, in the form of explosive spheres of energy.

And while looks alone are probably enough of a reason to like this Pokemon, here are a couple more reasons...
